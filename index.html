faq

Have Questions? We Have Answers!

Find solutions to your queries about membership, lesson schedules, and other SaddleUp services.

see all questions
What should I wear for my first riding lesson?
<

Wear long pants, a fitted shirt, and closed-toe shoes with a small heel. Avoid skirts, loose clothing, and sandals. Helmets are provided for your safety during lessons.

How do I book a riding lesson?

You can book a lesson through our website or contact us directly. Choose your preferred time and instructor. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak times.

Can I choose my own instructor?

Yes, you can choose your instructor based on their expertise and availability. We’ll do our best to match you with the right instructor during your booking.

What is the minimum age to start riding?

The minimum age to start riding is 7 years old. However, we recommend starting at an age where the child is comfortable with horses and can follow instructions safely.

Are there any discounts for long-term memberships?

Yes, we offer discounts for long-term memberships. The longer your commitment, the more you save. Please contact us for detailed pricing and available membership plans.

Do I need to bring my own horse for lessons?

No, you don’t need to bring your own horse. We provide well-trained horses for all lessons, ensuring that each rider is matched with an appropriate horse based on skill level.

What facilities are available for members?

Members have access to all our facilities, including the indoor and outdoor arenas, grooming stations, stables, and our equestrian shop. You can also enjoy our riding trails.

How do I cancel or reschedule my lesson?

To cancel or reschedule, simply contact us at least 24 hours in advance. You can reschedule to a different time or cancel without penalty within the allowed timeframe.

Is there a trial period for new members?

Yes, we offer a trial period for new members. This allows you to experience our programs and facilities before committing to a full membership. Please inquire for details on the trial duration.

Do you offer group riding lessons?

Yes, we offer group riding lessons for those who prefer learning in a group setting. Group lessons are a great way to socialize while improving riding skills. Check availability when booking.
